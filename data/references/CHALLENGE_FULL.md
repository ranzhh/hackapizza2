### Challenge Description

**Overview of the Challenge 💫**

In Cosmic Cycle 790, catering is no longer a simple commercial activity: it is one of the pillars of intergalactic balance.

Sentient species coming from distant stellar systems, orbital colonies, ancient civilizations, and dimensional travelers meet daily around a table… and they don't all eat the same way.

Each team controls a restaurant immersed in a living universe, where rare ingredients, unpredictable customers, and cultural constraints intertwine at every turn.

Managing a restaurant in the Multiverse means taking continuous decisions on:

* What to offer, and at what price, in a competitive market
* What to purchase or exchange, before the ingredients expire and become unusable
* What to cook and when, balancing time, capacity, and risk
* Who to serve, interpreting often ambiguous requests and respecting potentially lethal intolerances

Every mistake has a cost: a wasted ingredient is lost in the cosmic void, a customer served poorly or not served can damage the reputation of the restaurant well beyond a single turn and will not pay for the consumption!

> The final objective is to maximize the balance of the restaurant, a symbol of economic and managerial success.

But in the Gastronomic Multiverse of Cycle 790, it is not enough to know how to cook.

It counts to govern the chaos.

#### 1) The restaurants: what they are and what counts

Every restaurant is not just a place: it is a cultural outpost, a point of contact between different civilizations, an economic node observed with attention by the Federation.

Every restaurant is a complete entity with:

* **Balance:** the “cash” of the restaurant. It is the primary indicator of success and prestige.
* **Inventory:** the ingredients available at that moment, often coming from unstable commercial routes.
* **Menu:** the list of proposed recipes and the price of each.
* **Reputation:** the perception of the restaurant in the Multiverse, which influences the choice of customers.

#### Fundamental rule: perishable ingredients

In the cosmos nothing is eternal, and neither are ingredients.

📍 **The ingredients expire at the end of the turn**, victims of quantum instabilities, biological decay, or simply logistic routes that are too long.

This makes it crucial:

* to plan the purchase based on what one will actually be able to cook;
* to avoid spending on unused ingredients;
* to adapt the menu to what one actually has in hand, not to what one would wish to have.

#### 2) The turns: how a "day" of play flows

The game is divided into turns, each of which represents an operational day within the Cosmic Cycle.

Each turn marks the rhythm of the Gastronomic Multiverse: decisions taken too late or too early can alter the destiny of the restaurant.

#### 2.1 Speaking Phase – Planning and diplomacy

This phase represents the moment of negotiations behind closed doors.

The restaurants can:

* talk with the other teams;
* strike agreements and temporary alliances;
* negotiate exchanges and pacts of mutual interest.

It is here that great collaborations are born… and the most memorable betrayals.

> What you do NOT get here: ingredients from the system. The official routes are not yet open.

#### 2.2 Closed Bid Phase – Competitive procurement

When the official channels of the Federation open, the race for ingredients begins.

All restaurants participate in a blind auction: no one knows the offers of the others until the end.

Functional characteristics:

* every restaurant decides which ingredients it wants, in what quantity, and how much it is willing to pay;
* available ingredients are limited;
* whoever offers more has priority in obtaining the requested quantities;
* it is not guaranteed to obtain everything: you might receive only a part of what you requested;
* the prices of others become known only after the auction is concluded.

Here, it's not who spends the most who wins, but who spends the best.

#### 2.3 Waiting Phase – Settling and strategic setting

This is the phase of operational silence.

The restaurants:

* analyze what has been obtained;
* reorganize the kitchen;
* define the final menu;
* close or reinforce agreements.

It is the moment in which choices become irrevocable.

#### 2.4 Serving Phase – The service

The doors open and the customers arrive.

Now you will have to:

* correctly interpret requests;
* choose compatible dishes;
* cook and serve before time or ingredients run out.

> **Load management:** an overwhelmed restaurant can decide to close temporarily, protecting what remains.

#### 3) Opening and closing the restaurant

In the Multiverse there is no obligation to remain open.

Knowing when to close is a form of managerial wisdom.

* Staying open allows you to accumulate balance and reputation.
* Closing avoids operational collapse.
* A closed restaurant or one without a menu is invisible to customers.

#### 4) The menu: what the restaurant offers

The menu communicates the positioning of the restaurant in the gastronomic Multiverse.

Through the menu, the restaurant communicates who it addresses and who it intends to repel.

Prices, recipes, and food choices define your role in the Multiverse.

Setting a menu means:

* declaring which dishes the restaurant is willing to serve;
* establishing the price of each dish;
* contributing to determine the type of clientele that will frequent the restaurant.

#### Pricing Strategy

* **Low prices:** attract customers sensitive to cost but require high volume and great efficiency.
* **High prices:** aim for better margins, but risk driving away those with limited budgets.

#### 5) The recipes

Every recipe tells a story.

Some were born on agricultural planets, others in orbiting monasteries, others still in kitchens improvised during stellar wars.

The recipes determine ingredients, time, and prestige, and are the true engine of the culinary experience.

#### 6) The ingredients: scarce, variable, and perishable resource

Ingredients are rare, perishable, and often contested.

Possessing them means temporary power. Losing them means wasted opportunities.

#### 7) Intolerances and restrictions: in the multiverse not everyone eats the same way

In the Multiverse, a food error is not just a complaint.

It can be a diplomatic incident. Or worse.

Ignoring intolerances means risking lives, reputation, and federal sanctions.

Pay attention to the intolerances of customers. Do not serve them dishes that could have undesirable consequences.

#### 8) The customers: archetypes of the Multiverse

During the service, your restaurant is visited by customers coming from every corner of the universe. They are not simple patrons, but social archetypes, each with different expectations, constraints, and priorities.

What is the target of your restaurant? Everything depends on the dishes you have in your menu!

🚀 **Galactic Explorer**
Tireless traveler, always passing through between one wormhole and another.

* has little time
* has little budget
* quality is not a priority, as long as it is edible
* **What rewards:** simple, cheap, and very fast dishes.

💰 **Astrobaron**
Interstellar magnate, merchant of stars or lord of an asteroid belt.

* has very little time
* expects good dishes
* looks little at the price
* **What rewards:** quality, speed, and a menu that communicates status and prestige.

🔭 **Sages of the Cosmo**
Contemplative entities, scholars of taste and time, members of ancient orders.

* look for excellent dishes
* have time to waste
* care little about the price
* **What rewards:** prestigious recipes, rare ingredients, narrative and cultural coherence.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 **Orbital Families**
Evolved family units of the large space colonies.

* have a lot of time
* observe price and quality
* **What rewards:** balance between cost and value, well-prepared but accessible dishes, well-designed menu.

#### 9) The internal market: exchange between restaurants

The market between restaurants is a living ecosystem.

In addition to competitive procurement, restaurants can exchange with each other.

The market is public and visible to all restaurants. Being attentive and reactive can lead to great gains! Don't wait for other restaurants to steal the best ingredients from you!

What it allows functionally:

* buying missing ingredients when procurement was not enough;
* selling surplus that would otherwise expire;
* building economic alliances (or traps).

Offers are canceled at the end of each turn.

#### 10) Chronicles from the cosmos: news, events and controlled instability

Stay Tuned…

#### 11) How to win

Winner is who manages to:

* transform limited ingredients into dishes served with continuity;
* read requests well and reduce errors/waste;
* manage competition and market with timing;
* protect the balance by avoiding useless purchases and wasted ingredients;
* earn more at the end of the challenge!

### Technical Specifications ⚙️

This guide describes how a team interfaces with the server during the competition.

Below you find a Python template called `client_template.py` with a skeleton already prepared for connection to the server (SSE and basic event management). You can use it as a starting point to build your solution.

*Set the api key and the team ID that will be provided to you by the organization.*

*Launch the python file with `uv run client_template.py` or install dependencies with `pip install aiohttp` and launch the script with `python3 client_template.py*`

#### Access and authentication

All team calls require the header `x-api-key`.
The same API key must be used for HTTP, SSE, and MCP.
If the key is not valid, the response is `401 Unauthorized`.

#### Mandatory framework: Datapizza AI

The solution must be implemented using the Datapizza AI framework. It is the open-source framework developed by the Datapizza R&D Team to build Gen AI solutions - agents, RAG, and orchestration.

We are actively working on the agentic kernel of the framework: your use during the challenge, the customizations, and extensions you apply will give us precious insights to improve it. Consider Hackapizza 2.0 also as an opportunity to contribute to the evolution of the framework.

`pip install datapizza-ai`

📖 Documentation: [docs.datapizza.ai/0.0.9](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://docs.datapizza.ai/0.0.9/)

#### Turns and phases

Turns are started by the organizers.
A turn goes through the phases in this order:
`speaking` → `closed_bid` → `waiting` → `serving` → `stopped`.

The phase transition and the turn start must be read from the SSE events (`game_started`, `game_phase_changed`).

Durations can vary during the event: do not make rigid assumptions on times, always follow SSE.

**Endpoint**
Method: `GET`

Initial handshake: `data: connected`

### Run Schedule and Game Turns 🗓️

The competition is divided into different phases, each with distinct rules and objectives. The key concept is the **Run**: a continuous session composed of multiple consecutive game turns.

Runs can be of three types:

1. **Testing Run:** results are not counted. The game can be reset at the discretion of the organizers.
2. **Official Run:** results count towards the score.
3. **Golden Run:** the final and decisive run, in which only agents work without any intervention on the code.

> ⚠️ **Note on turn cadence:** each game turn lasts approximately 5-7 minutes, but the frequency and density of turns within a run are variable and at the discretion of the organizers. Do not make rigid assumptions: always follow SSE events and official communication channels (Discord).

### Saturday

#### 🕐 12:00 – 14:00 | Study of the track and setup

The server will be active and reachable. You can start contacting routes, exploring endpoints, and familiarizing yourself with the infrastructure. No active run.

#### 🕑 14:00 – 17:00 | Testing Run

Starting with the first game runs. Results are NOT counted. Game can be reset at any time.

#### 🕔 17:00 – Sunday 10:00 | Official Run

From this moment results count towards the score. Single prolonged run with numerous turns.

* Game is NOT reset.
* You can modify code between turns.
* Frequency communicated via Discord/IRL.

### Sunday

#### 🔟 10:00 | 🛑 Stop the Coding

No more code modifications possible. Complete game reset.

#### 🕙 10:00 – 12:00 | Golden Run 🏆

Final and decisive run. Only agents work. No intervention allowed. Results have important weight on final ranking.
